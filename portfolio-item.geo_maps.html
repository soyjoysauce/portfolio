Final Product:

For our project we were encouraged to use at least three API(Application Programming Interfaces) to build a site based on our targeted audience who were children between the age of 7-9 who needs a interactive educational tools. We Assembled : YouTube, GeoCharts, RESTCountries Data as our API of choice. We built a one-page website that integrates all three API to teach 7-9 year olds to click in any landmass from GeoCharts and its user is shown a modal with Youtube and Data regarding the country clicked.
