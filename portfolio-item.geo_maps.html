Final Product:

Our website will be based on our targeted audience: children between the age of 7-9 who needs a interactive educational tools.
API(Application Programming Interfaces) used: YouTube, GeoCharts, RESTCountries Data.
We built a one-page website integrating all three API's for our targeted audience to learn about geography.
A guessing game was also added to the wesite for a fun interactive way to test ones knowledge.
