Dev Tools

HTML, CSS, JavaScript , PHP, MySQL

Final Product:

BoardGameScout is a social networking site to allow board game enthusiasts to find other like-minded people to join them for Board Game events in their own home, or in locations in the community.

Members can either host events, or seek out events in their area to apply for, and hosts may choose applicant whom they feel will mesh well with their group.

Privacy is a main concern for us, and personal information for location and contact is only supplied to applicants after a host accepts their application.
