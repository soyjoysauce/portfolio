Final Product:

Board Game Scout was a final project during my last six weeks of the final project. Board Game Scout was created out of the intention of filling the need of board game enthusiasts to find fellow gamers at a short notice. The host is able to create a event that is invitation only but gives the possible attendees the general area of the event to give distance estimation. We are hoping to expand more on what kind of things users can do to customize their experience. For now let's be board not bored. A mobile friendly app with integrated Facebook o-auth to give users seamless access to the app. Host is able to create and post events and receive emails regarding their events through our php mailer. Participants are able to apply for the game and receive notification when their application was accepted or not. Participants are able to have their favorite genre of games as their profile token of choice in profile page. We are hoping to get this into a market where people can meet new people while trying out new games. This provides advertisement platform for board game companies who can sponsor events or reviews up on our blogs from players.
